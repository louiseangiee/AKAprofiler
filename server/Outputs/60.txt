BY SENIOR OFFICIAL 1, UNMIL 
 
 
1. The Investigations Division of the Office of Int ernal Oversight Services 
(ID/OIOS) received information that a Senior Offici al for the United Nations Mission in 
Liberia (SRSG UNMIL) was involved in business dealin gs with a businessman operating

benefit of either himself or of the businessman.
